Chicken Invaders

Made By Omar Wattad 207511759, Shady Dhamshy 206989527, Abed Awaisy 316445626

Usages

  • Each time the enemy hits a wall they move down (closer to our ship).
  • If our ship hits a bad ship, it will die immediately (losing all lives).
  • We have a custom time that the player can set.
  • Each time we hit an enemy, it drops meat (prize) that we need to collect to increase our score (killing chickens will not increase the score).
  • A prize drops every 5 seconds, giving us 10 points when collected.

Difficulties

  • Fast enemy movement.
  • Multiple enemies on the screen at once.
  • Small player ship size.
  • Custom time increases difficulty.
  • Bad ships that move faster and are harder to avoid.
